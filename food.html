Summer Flavors

From family feasts to sweet summer treats, these are the unforgettable dishes that made my summer truly delicious.

PASTRY

Strawberry Creme Filled Croissant

4.5

Flaky, buttery croissant generously filled with smooth strawberry crème. A delightful pastry that combines the freshness of strawberries with rich, creamy sweetness—perfect for a summer morning treat.

Pastry Strawberry Sweet
RAMEN

Kyu Kyu Ramen

3.5

The ramen was good considering its affordable price compared to other ramens. The broth was flavorful and rich, with a comforting warmth that made it perfect for a rainy summer day.

Noodles Japanese Soup
SANDWICH

Club House Sandwich

4.0

A triple-layered sandwich stacked with ham, chicken, egg, lettuce, tomato, and cheese. Perfectly toasted and served with a side of fries—ideal for sharing with friends on a sunny afternoon.

Sandwich Savory Snack
PIZZA

Spinach Pizza w/ Cookie Butter Latte

5.0

A unique summer combo—crispy thin-crust pizza topped with creamy spinach and cheese, paired with a sweet, nutty cookie butter latte. The perfect balance of savory and sweet for a memorable meal.

Pizza Savory Latte Unique Combo
KOREAN BBQ

Samgyeopsal

4.0

Juicy slices of grilled pork belly, cooked right at the table and wrapped in lettuce with garlic, ssamjang, and fresh veggies. A fun, interactive meal that brings friends and family together for a true Korean BBQ experience.

Korean BBQ Grilled Pork
PASTA

Baked Mac Lasagna

4.5

Layers of macaroni and rich meat sauce, topped with creamy cheese and baked to perfection. This hearty pasta dish is a festival favorite—comforting, cheesy, and perfect for sharing with family during celebrations.
